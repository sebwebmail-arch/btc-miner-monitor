Continuity SLA — by Datacenter ⏱ Every 30 min
How it's calculated
At each 30-min snapshot: r = workers_online / workers_active — score = r² (quadratic penalty).
workers_active excludes workers whose last snapshot is >7 days before the window end — stale machines don't count against the DC.
Continuity SLA = average of all snapshot scores × 100. Large outages are penalised heavily; small fractions are lenient:
99% → 98.0% 95% → 90.3% 90% → 81.0% 80% → 64.0% 70% → 49.0% 50% → 25.0%

Account Performance — ATO ⏱ Every 30 min
How it's calculated
ATO (Actual Transfer Out) = hashrate cmine commits to deliver to clients. If total HR ≥ ATO, clients receive 100%.
At each snapshot: snapshot_SLA = min(1, total_hr / ATO)
period_SLA = average(snapshot_SLAs) × 100
This combines availability and throughput — snapshots where HR = 0 score 0%.

DC Performance — Throughput & Breach Responsibility ⏱ Every 30 min
How it's calculated
DC share: avg_hr_dc / avg_total_hr computed only on online snapshots — avoids downtime deflating the share.
Nominal at ts: total_hr_ts × DC_share — expected DC contribution relative to account total (not ATO).
Throughput (when online): avg(min(1, hr_dc_ts / nominal_at_ts)) — does the DC consistently deliver its proportional share?
Combined SLA: Continuity × Throughput — overall delivery including downtime.
Breach responsibility: during snapshots where total_hr < ATO (client impact), was this DC below its nominal? Lower = better. 0% = DC never contributed to client shortfalls.
